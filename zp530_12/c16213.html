Atlantic Jazz Legends

UK 20 CD box set containing 20 original Jazz albums from the iconic Atlantic Records label released between 1958 and 1970. The box includes classic albums from Jazz legends such as John Coltrane, Charles Mingus, Mose Allison, Freddie Hubbard, Ornette Coleman, Keith Jarrett, Modern Jazz Quartet, Herbie Mann, Chick Corea, Thelonious Monk and many others. These are albums that helped shape Jazz music and continue to be an influence on new generations of musicians and music lovers.
